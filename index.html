The Philosophical Failure: Breaking the Von Neumann Bottleneck

There has been sentiment of lament over Israel’s supposed failure to invest in the AI space. “Why isn’t there an Israeli ChatGPT?” one might ask. This is unfair as a lot of the R&D is happening in Israel. Consider NVIDIA’s significant presence in Israel or Google DeepMind’s Israeli research team. Not to mention the work done by universities and startups in this space as well as the Ministry of Defense’s dedicated AI unit. One could argue that ChatGPT is the Israeli ChatGPT.
